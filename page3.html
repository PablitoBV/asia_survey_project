Explanation of the variables:

averageAge: The average age of the respondents

parentsPlacement: Average placement of the respondent's parents on a scale from 1 to 10 (from poorest to richest)

selfPlacement: Average placement of the respondent themself on a scale from 1 to 10 (from poorest to richest)

childrenPlacement: Average placement of the respondent's children on a scale from 1 to 10 (from poorest to richest)

yearsEducation: Average number of years of education

incomeQuintile: Average quintile for the respondent's income
